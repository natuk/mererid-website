K Bloc - MA Final Show

Submitted by mererid on Tue, 09/11/2018 - 09:35

MA ARTS PRACTICE (FINE ART and ARTS IN HEALTH & WELLBEING) FINAL SHOW

Launch event - Friday 14th September 5pm - 8pm
(show continues daily 10am - 4pm until 19th Sept.)

Kidwelly Building, University of South Wales, Treforest Campus, CF37 1DL

I will be one of 11 artists presenting their final MA show at K Bloc this September. Amongst paintings and mixed media work expect to find a meadow, a laboratory, a forest clearing and dystopian shop. Issues engaged with include loss, healing, privacy in the information age, social class and taste.

Exhibiting artists:
Nichola Goff
Kate Hearnden
Miira Hyvonen
Nick Jenkins
Rachel Jones
Lucille Junkere
Rebecca Lewis
Sarah Stebbing
Zoe Louise Ridgway
Mererid Velios
Amber Jay Williams

All welcome to the opening on the 14th and I will be invigilating on the 15th. Hope to see you there!
