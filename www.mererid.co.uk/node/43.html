LoL = Lexicon of Lust

Submitted by mererid on Tue, 05/22/2018 - 12:38

One of my works, 'Whites colour chart', was accepted into this exhibition for Fringe arts Bath, opening this Friday evening. Since the work has proved popular I decided to make a limited edition of 11.

This was the callout/description of the exhibition:
LoL = Lexicon of Lust
In Art we feel that meaning has become meaningless. Or do we - perhaps we don't mean that? We welcome work that is playful, ambiguous, theatrical, dystopian, humorous, contrarian and perhaps even alludes to the figurative. A good title will go a long way. No paintings, drawings or performances thank you - just 'things' that we can 'lust' over.

Where: FaB 1, 94-96 Walcot Street, Bath BA1 5BG
Launch: 25 May open 6pm 'til late - #FaB18 Opening Night Arty Party
When: 10am to 6pm daily - 26 May to 10 June
