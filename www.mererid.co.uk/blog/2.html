Interior Monologues

Submitted by mererid on Thu, 05/16/2019 - 22:13

A group show at Oriel y Bont with many layers to it. Contributing artists responded to an artwork in the Art Collection of the University of South Wales. They also needed to provide at least 4 pages of artwork for an A5 zine to go with the show. Creative writers were assigned to each artist and wrote something in response to both the new and old work. At the exhibition opening each writer read their piece of writing. Hearing Maria Lalic read the text she wrote in response to my work and our conversations via email was a highlight for me.

'The greatest dilemma of all time' - now open

Submitted by mererid on Mon, 06/04/2018 - 21:30

The exhibition is now open at Art in the Attic and will be shown until June 15th.
These photos give you a sense of the space and the work but without the sound. The long wall-based work is accompanied by a sound work of a voice reciting the 320 paint colour names on a loop.

Introduction text:
THE GREATEST DILEMMA OF ALL TIME

Mererid Velios’s exhibition is a playful, quietly subversive exploration of the designer paint colour chart and what paint companies’ marketing strategies reveals about our society today.
